🌌 Exploration
🔬 Science & Tech

🛰 NASA

The United States’ space agency responsible for Apollo missions, Mars rovers, and the James Webb Space Telescope.

🇮🇳 ISRO

India’s space agency known for Mangalyaan, Chandrayaan, and the upcoming Gaganyaan mission.

🚀 SpaceX

Founded by Elon Musk, developed Falcon 9, Starship, Starlink, and more.

🌕 Moon

Earth’s only natural satellite, visited during Apollo and targeted by Artemis and Chandrayaan missions.

🔴 Mars

The red planet, home to Perseverance and Curiosity rovers, a future human destination.

🌞 Solar System

Includes the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. The foundation of our cosmic neighborhood.

🪐 Exoplanets

Planets that orbit stars outside our solar system. Thousands have been discovered using telescopes like Kepler and TESS.

👨‍🚀 Astronauts

Trained space travelers who conduct missions aboard spacecraft like the ISS, Artemis Orion, and Soyuz.

🌌 Deep Space

Beyond our solar system lies deep space — filled with galaxies, quasars, black holes, and cosmic mysteries.

🔭 Telescopes

Instruments that observe distant celestial objects. Famous examples include Hubble, JWST, and ALMA.

🛰 Satellites

Machines in orbit used for communication, Earth observation, weather, science, and navigation (like GPS).

🧬 Astrobiology

The study of life in the universe. Explores conditions for life on other planets and origins of life on Earth.

🤖 AI

Artificial Intelligence helps analyze space data, control rovers, and manage autonomous missions in real time.

🚀 Rockets

Launch vehicles like Saturn V, Falcon 9, and GSLV carry spacecraft into orbit or beyond Earth.

⚖️ Space Law

International laws and treaties that govern outer space activities and ensure peaceful use beyond Earth.

🛸 Virgin Galactic

A private spaceflight company focused on suborbital space tourism using the SpaceShipTwo system.
